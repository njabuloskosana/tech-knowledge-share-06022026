## The Framework (One Last Time)

1. **Be Specific**  Clear goals beat vague wishes
2. **Add Structure**  Guide the AI's output format
3. **Give Examples**  Show, don't just tell
4. **Work in Steps**  Break big tasks into small ones
5. **Refine & Repeat**  Iteration beats perfection
